## Tone of Voice

### DO:
- Lead with the outcome: *"₦223,000 in extra revenue"* not *"we helped improve their earnings"*
- Use specifics: *"twice a week, year-round"* not *"regular buyers"*
- Sound confident without being arrogant
- Use the client/buyer's perspective — what's in it for them?
- Short sentences. Strong verbs. No filler.

### DON'T:
- Use buzzwords: *"synergise"*, *"leverage"*, *"holistic approach"*
- Make promises without data to back them up
- Sound corporate or stiff
- Understate results — if the numbers are impressive, say so
