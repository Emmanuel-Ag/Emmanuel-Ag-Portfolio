## Revenue Uplift Formula

```
Revenue @ Old Price  = Total Qty × Old Price/kg
Revenue @ New Price  = Total Qty × New Price/kg
Uplift (₦)          = New Revenue − Old Revenue
Uplift (%)          = (New Revenue − Old Revenue) ÷ Old Revenue × 100
```

- [ ] Buyer misses more than 2 consecutive scheduled purchases

- [ ] Supply volume drops below what buyers need (farm-side risk)
- [ ] New competitor approaches your buyer with a lower price
