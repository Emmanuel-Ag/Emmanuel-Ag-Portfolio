# 📨 Buyer Outreach Templates
**Lingr Network — Scripts for Approaching Premium Buyers**

*Adapt these to the specific product, buyer, and context. Never send a generic message.*

---

## Template 1 — Restaurant / Food Business (WhatsApp or In-Person)

> **Use when:** Approaching a restaurant, hotel kitchen, caterer, or food brand that uses the product regularly.

---

**Opening Message:**

"Good [morning/afternoon], my name is [Your Name] from Lingr Network. We supply [product] on behalf of a greenhouse farm here in [location].

We noticed [restaurant name] would likely go through a significant volume of [product] weekly, and we'd love to offer you a consistent, reliable supply at a competitive price.

Our product is [key differentiator — e.g. greenhouse-grown, consistent sizing, no pesticides]. We can deliver [X kg] twice a week, guaranteed.

Would you be open to a quick call or a trial delivery so you can see the quality for yourself?"

---

**Follow-Up (if no response after 2 days):**

"Just following up on my message about [product] supply. We're currently onboarding a few restaurant partners and wanted to make sure you had the chance to be included.

Happy to arrange a free sample delivery — no commitment needed. Just let me know a convenient time."

---

## Template 2 — Reseller / Wholesaler

> **Use when:** Approaching a market trader, wholesaler, or distributor who resells to end consumers.

---

**Opening Message:**

"Hello [Name], I'm reaching out from Lingr Network. We represent a greenhouse farm producing [product] at consistent volumes.

We're looking for reseller partners who can move [X kg] weekly. Our pricing is [₦X/kg], and we can guarantee supply [frequency].

The quality is consistent — greenhouse-grown, same size and freshness every time — which makes it easier for you to sell at a premium to your customers.

Can we arrange a meeting or a sample this week?"

---

**Price Negotiation Response (when buyer pushes back on price):**

"I understand — pricing matters. What I can tell you is that our quality is consistent, which means less waste on your end and happier customers.

We're not the cheapest option, and we're not trying to be. But we are the most reliable, and in [product], reliability is everything. 

Let's do a two-week trial at [₦X/kg]. If you're not satisfied with the quality and consistency, we'll talk. I'm confident you will be."

---

## Template 3 — Cold Introduction at Market or Trade Event

> **Use when:** Meeting potential buyers in person at a market, trade fair, or industry event.

---

**In-Person Pitch (30-second version):**

"Hi, I'm [Name] from Lingr Network. We supply greenhouse-grown [product] to restaurants and resellers in [city]. We focus on consistent quality and guaranteed supply — no shortfalls, no surprises. Are you currently sourcing [product]? I'd love to tell you more."

## Template 5 — Referral Ask (from existing buyer)

> **Use when:** A satisfied buyer can introduce you to another potential buyer.

---

**Message:**

"[Buyer Name], really glad the supply has been working well for you. We're actually expanding our buyer network this quarter and I wanted to ask — do you know any other [restaurants/resellers/businesses] who might benefit from the same arrangement?

Any introduction would mean a lot, and of course we'd make sure they get the same quality and reliability you've experienced."

---

## Key Rules for All Outreach

1. **Personalise every message** — mention the buyer's specific business by name
2. **Lead with what's in it for them** — reliability, quality, convenience — not just price
3. **Always offer a low-risk next step** — a sample, a trial, a call
4. **Follow up twice** before moving on — most deals close on follow-up, not the first message
5. **Anchor the price confidently** — never apologise for premium pricing
